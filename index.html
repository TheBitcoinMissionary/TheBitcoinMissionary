💡 Bitcoin was launched in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto.
🔐 Bitcoin is secured by cryptographic algorithms.
🌍 Bitcoin is a decentralized global currency.
⛏️ New bitcoins are created by mining.
📜 The total supply is capped at 21 million.
⚡ Transactions are verified by a global network.
🧱 Each block contains a cryptographic hash of the previous block.
🧠 Bitcoin empowers financial sovereignty.
